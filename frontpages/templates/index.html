ABOUT US

The Billiard Academy League is a web based application that allows League Operators to efficiently execute leagues. The application allows the registration of players who can be easily included into your league at the click of a mouse. It also tracks the players handicaps with a proven algorithm to all players. In addition, it provides players with reports about their previous matches and information on upcoming leagues.

For League Operators, it's a complete system for executing an in-house league or in conjunction with other local or non-local Operators. The system provides, scheduling, handicaps, reports, and player registration. It also provides access to a database that includes all the registered players.
